01

General overview

This document presents the reconstruction of the family tree of the TEDA (FOTIE) family, traced across 7 generations from the founders TEFOUETZAP & MAFOKO down to DEMANOU Donald (b. 1994). The central pivot ancestor is TEDA (FOTIE), born around 1870, who had four wives and many descendants.

The information was reconstructed from handwritten notes. This edition v2 (20 June 2026) incorporates the oral corrections of 13 June 2026 and the attachment of the eleven members of the "extended branch" to TSANA Sébastien, confirmed on 20 June 2026.
